FIFA is a series of association football simulation video games developed and released annually by Electronic Arts under the EA Sports label. The Guardian called the series "the slickest, most polished and by far the most popular football game around". As of 2011, the FIFA franchise has been localised into 18 languages and available in 51 countries. Listed in Guinness World Records as the best-selling sports video game franchise in the world, by 2019, the FIFA series had sold over 282.4 million copies. It is also one of the best-selling video game franchises.
